1. Open the app

On your iPhone or laptop, open Safari or Edge and go to:
https://anameen123.github.io/marketing-planner/marketing_schedule_FINAL4.html

Pro tip — add it to your iPhone home screen:

  1. Open the link in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button (square with up-arrow at the bottom)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add
Now you have a one-tap app icon — opens full-screen with no browser bar.
Bookmark on laptop: press Ctrl+D after the page loads.
The sign-in screen

Enter your work email + Outlook password

You have two ways to sign in. Pick whichever is faster for you:

  1. Sign in with Microsoft button at the top — opens the standard Microsoft pop-up where you enter your work email and password.
  2. Email + Password fields below — type your email and Outlook password directly. Microsoft will verify them on their secure page.

Use the same email + password you use for Outlook:

3. After signing in — the Home screen

You land on the Home tab. You'll see:
  • Your name & circle avatar in the top header
  • A "Good afternoon/morning" welcome card with today's date
  • Any announcements from the admin (red banner)
  • Bottom navigation with all the main tabs
  • Sign out button next to your name when you're done
Live sync: everything you do here syncs automatically to the whole team within 5 seconds. No "save" button needed.

5. Schedule a visit

1Tap the Dashboard tab at the bottom.

2You see a calendar. Days with planned visits show a small number badge and a colored dot.

3Tap the day you want to schedule. The day opens showing 6 visit slots.

4In any empty row, type the clinic name (autocomplete will suggest from the bank), or tap "+ Add Visit" for a new one.

5Your name auto-fills as the marketer. You're scheduled.

To add a visit for a clinic that doesn't exist yet: add it to the Businesses tab first, then come back to the Dashboard to schedule.
Calendar — dots & numbers show planned visits

6. Mark a visit complete & log spending

The day view — your visits in a list
After your visit, come back and update the row:
  1. Open the day on the Dashboard (tap the day cell)
  2. Tap the pencil ✏ next to your visit
  3. Set status: Completed / Postponed / Canceled
  4. If you spent money (lunch, gift, cupcakes…), tap Log Spending → enter amount + what for
  5. If you established a relationship, set the Lead level: L1 (Contact) → L2 (Receptionist) → L3 (Office Manager) → L4 (Owner / Decision-maker)
Spending limits: the app shows your per-visit cap and remaining quarterly budget. Stay within them — no admin approval needed.
Important: mark visits the same day if you can. The team uses your real-time updates to plan their week.

7. Browse clinics, MVA, & outreach

The Businesses tab is your bank of targets — every clinic, attorney firm, and community org we know about.
  • Three sub-tabs: Clinics, MVA Attorneys, Community Outreach
  • Use the search box to find one by clinic or doctor name
  • Filter by Lead Status, Area (city), or Specialty (PCP, Pediatrics…)
  • Each card shows: doctor name, specialty, lead status, tier (Bronze / Silver / Gold / Platinum), and referrals this period
  • Tap "+ Set Lead" on a card to update your relationship level inline

Need to add a brand-new clinic? Tap the purple "+ Add Clinic" button at the top of the list.

Tier badges: Bronze / Silver / Gold / Platinum show how many referrals a clinic has sent us this period. Higher tier = bigger spending cap when you visit.
595 clinics, searchable & filterable

8. Sign out when you're done

Tap "Sign out" at the top-right of any page. You're signed out immediately — no waiting, no extra pop-up.

On a shared device? Always sign out before handing it back. On your personal phone, you can stay signed in — the app remembers you for next time.
