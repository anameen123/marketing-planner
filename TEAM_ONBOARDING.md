# 🚀 Welcome to the new Marketing Planner

A web-based replacement for the old paper-and-spreadsheet workflow. Schedule visits, log spending, track referrals — everyone on the team sees the same data, live.

---

## 📱 First-time setup — 2 minutes

### Open the app

The admin (Mahmoud) will send you a link. It looks something like:

> `https://anameen123.github.io/marketing-planner/marketing_schedule_FINAL4.html`

Or, after a custom domain is set up:

> `https://marketing.iccoftexas.com`

Open it in **Safari** (iPhone) or **Edge / Chrome** (laptop).

### Sign in

1. You'll see a white **"Sign in with Microsoft"** button at the top
2. Tap / click it
3. A Microsoft sign-in window opens
4. Enter your `@wcgtx.com` email and the password you use for Outlook
5. Approve the **multi-factor authentication** prompt on your phone (text or app)
6. You'll see a permission screen: **"Marketing Planner wants to access SharePoint and your profile"** → tap **Accept**
7. The app loads with your name and role at the top right

### iPhone — add to Home Screen (recommended)

After signing in once:

1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari (the box with the up-arrow)
2. Scroll down → tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Tap **Add**

You now have a real-looking app icon on your home screen. **Tap it any time to open the app — already signed in, no Safari URL bar, fullscreen.**

## 🔄 Live sync — see what your team is doing

When **anyone** on the team makes a change — assigns themselves a visit, logs spending, updates a lead — **everyone else sees it within 5 seconds**. No refresh needed.

The little 🟢 **Live** badge at the top-right of the screen tells you the live-sync is active. If it shows ❌ **Sync error**, click it for help.

---

## 🔔 The bell — when admin makes changes to your work

If the admin edits one of YOUR visits, leads, or spending, you'll get a notification in the bell at the top-right. Click it to see what was changed and when.

Other members' notifications stay private to them.

## 🚨 Things to know

### Editing rules
- You can edit your visits within **2 days after the visit date**
- After that, only the admin can edit (and they're notified you'll see the change)
- "Completed" can't be marked before the visit date
- Visits can't be rescheduled to the past

### Spending rules
- Each business has a **per-visit spending cap** (Bronze / Silver / Gold / Platinum tiers)
- Each business also has a **quarterly budget pool**
- Going over either limit needs admin approval (you'll see a popup with the rules)

### Quarterly resets
- Every 3 months, business tiers get re-ranked based on patient referrals
- Spending pools reset
- Lifetime counters (Random + Holiday outreach) never reset

## 🆘 Help

- **Can't sign in?** Tell admin (Mahmoud) — your email might not be in the access list yet
- **App is slow?** Refresh once. If still slow, the SharePoint sync might be hiccuping — wait 30 seconds and try again
- **Forgot to do something?** Just open the app — the live sync means you can see what's been done team-wide
- **Found a bug?** Tell admin, who can fix or escalate
